How to Print Our Connect the Dots Worksheets

Printing Instructions

  1. Click the PDF or PNG download button below your chosen design
  2. Save the file to your computer or device
  3. Open the file with a PDF viewer or image viewer
  4. Select "Print" from the file menu
  5. Adjust settings as needed (see recommendations)
  6. Click "Print" to print your connect the dots worksheet

Recommended Settings

  • Use actual size (100%) in print settings
  • Choose portrait orientation for best results
  • For younger kids, print at 125% scale
